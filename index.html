Ken (今和泉謙, Imaizumi Ken, born 27 January 2008) is a student, who studies at European International School and plays football for Fox Football Vietnam.[5][6]

Early years

[edit]

Born in Fukuoka Japan, Ken moved to Vietnam at the age of 3 and has been living in Ho Chi Minh City since. He has been enrolling in European International School since the first grade and continues to do so in DP. This also means that he has been close friends with many students here that have stayed at this school for a long duration. Thus, his language acquisition in English is superior to his Japanese language acquisition due to studying English deeper, on the other hand, his Vietnamese acquisition remains stagnant since the first day of his stay here. He lives in Ho Chi Minh City with his parents, noting that he is the only child in his family. Though, during the holidays, he often returns back to Japan to visit family and relatives residing in Hiroshima and Nagasaki. [9]

During Covid, when country completely shut down, He and his family decided to return back to Japan which has more lenient restrictions on Covid as it had gotten quite bad at this point with 1-2 years of no school. They went to a small town in Japan called Hakuba, Nagano and enrolled a Junior High School there.As one could expect, he did not understand any of the material he was learning there. A famous activity of Hakuba is skiing, so since there was no point in learning nothing at the school, he decided to ski for the ski season instead. He was able to improve his ski skills quite drastically in this time period.'[23]

Hobbies

[edit]

Ken often spends his time playing football. He has joined various team in the past including men's teams like Sporting Legends, and Saigon Raiders more recently and the Fox Football Team.[10] He also enjoys watching media such as Youtube and movies/TV shows with the most recent being Brooklyn 99. His preferred music includes the likes of the Marias, The Cardiagans or Yuika. Favourite food includes anything Japanese.[8]
